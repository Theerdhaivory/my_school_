Our Campus

SEP Core Team

B.D.M.I. is highly mindful of the safety concerns of its students. In order to ensure maximum safety and various measures are carried out by the School. ISO & OHSAS implementation team for School Excellence Program (SEP) has been formed, comprising of selected members of administration, teaching & other support staff.

Disaster Management Team

ERT Team – We have a designated disaster management team which analyzes risk areas and takes swift action for prevention of imminent risk areas. Our team of members undergo regular training to handle any unforeseen situation.

Infirmary

Our infirmary is equipped with all the medical facilities required to combat any emergency situation in the School. It is well managed by experienced and trained nurses.

Essentials - Power / RO

We have two RO plants and the entire building is supported with generator facility with 24-hour power backup and supply.

green Campus

Green Campus We have an environmental friendly green campus designed for a learning experience that will prepare our students to lead the world towards a healthier, cleaner, and more sustainable future.

IEE Proyog award


IEE Proyog Award 2022

A remarkable feat has been achieved by the students of B.D.M. International in a science project competition organized by the Electrical Engineering Department, Jadavpur University on 21.10.2022. Tannistha Debnath, Swapneel Bose and Debopam Roychowdhury of XI SC C from our school have secured the first position and bagged a prize money of Rs.3000 along with certificates of achievement. The students had made the project on “Use of Ionic Wind as a Coolant”. This novel project was highly appreciated by the judges, who also lauded the other innovative working models made by the students from our school.

Laboratories

  • Science lab

    Our futuristic Science labs are equipped with all modern facilities and equipment to encourage active and experiential learning. Using interesting, relevant and informative tools has had great results in simplifying advanced science concepts.

  • Maths Lab

    Our state-of-the-art Math labs exist in Primary and Upper Primary sections to facilitate real life experience. Use of animations, gamification, tool kits and creative instruction, and explanation, Not only engages the learner, but also have the potential for greater concept retention.

  • Geography Lab

    Our state-of-the-art Geo labs are engaging, so that the subjects can truly “come alive” for them. They comprise of map reading boards, materials for extensive learning for Class XI-XII students.

  • Robotics lab

    Robotics is a fun and effective way to introduce kids to not only programming but also to engage their interests in other subjects in a creative manner. It makes kids learn Science and Math in engaging ways and can capture a child’s imagination like no other tool by creating a fun and learning process. Our Robotics labs are futuristic and have ample facilities and equipment to enable students with inquiry-based learning.

  • AI lab

    Modern learning is about collaborating with others, solving complex problems and critical thinking. Our tech kids are the future digital citizens who can transform our country into a digitally empowered society. Our A.I. labs are futuristic and have ample facilities and equipment.

  • IT lab

    Our modern IT Lab prepares students for the future. Meticulously planned and equipped with unmatched hardware and software, updated to their latest versions, we have a 1:1 student computer ratio.

  • language lab

    We have Language and Composite Laboratories which are integral to the development of linguistic skills and cultivating expression.

  • Financial Market Mgmt.

    The Finance Market Management Lab has a goal for our students to gain knowledge in finance as well as learn life lessons in money management. Each week we go over basic financial terms, concepts, and information regarding the business world.
